The Power of Superfoods!

What is a Superfood?

I love nourishing my body with food that I know is improving my health and overall wellbeing. I feel good when I eat good, and wanted to share my knowledge on my favorite superfoods (while simulateously practicing jquery ;) ).

  • Acai - high in antioxidants!
  • Cacao - contains flavanoids which helps lower blood pressure!
  • Chia Seeds - packed with omega-3s, fiber, and calcium!
  • Hemp Seeds - high in protein and contain all the essential amino acids needed for growth and repair!
  • Spirulina - an algae that is high in protein and an excellent source of vitamins!
  • Spinach or Kale - lots and lots of vitamins and minerals!

The best part about superfoods is that they're super easy to incorporate into your diet. I eat pretty much all of what's on the list above daily. I put spinach, kale, or spirulina into smoothies (I don't love salads) and you can't even taste the difference. I put cacao in my smoothies, and they instantly tast like chocolate milkshakes, but it's good for you! I top my oatmeal with chia and hemp seeds as an added boost of protein and healthy fats. And I make acai bowls and top with lots of fruit and granola.
